Our Mission

AuralVision develops and provides inexpensive glasses for the sight impaired which convert visual depth into stereo sound, creating an innovative way to experience and navigate the world.

Current Progress

Proof of Concept Achievement

We have successfully developed and built a working proof-of-concept, featuring three time-of-flight (ToF) laser sensors, bone-conduction stereo headphones to keep the ears uncovered, rechargeable battery pack and custom Python software for depth-to-audio conversion. Next steps include adding two more ToF sensors for broader width of object detection, and an accelerometer to mute sounds when the glasses are kept still, allowing the user to more easily hear conversations and background sounds.
